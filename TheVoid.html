Note: For the clarification of ambiguity, Void refers to the being, Void refers to the substance, and The Void refers to the location.

Section 1 / Description:

Void is an approximately 6 foot tall humanoid* creature of unknown origin.

Her body is made of a malleable substance she refers to as Void.

Void can shapeshift at her own will, i.e. squishiness when she's hugged.

Void normally feels somewhat silky and soft, although Void can also control this. They can also adjust the friction of Void.

Section 3 / Abilities:

Void has complete control over all molecules in her body, allowing her to do things like floating or taking off a limb as a party trick.

Adding to this, Void can clone herself, allowing for her to be in multiple places at once.

With the ability to clone herself comes her pet, Abyss. Abyss is not always the same animal, having different forms, similarly to Void.

Void does not need to eat, nor can she feel pain.

Void is able to use a "rift" to travel between our dimension, and The Void, her homeland. The distances between the rifts in mortal space and The Void can vary, allowing for pseudo-teleportation.

These rifts may be anywhere from very large to molecular size, and can vary in amount, depending on her goals.

Void is also able to use these rifts to see in places that she isn't physically.

Section 4 / The Void

The Void as a place is very barron. The ground is a practically infinite mass of idle Void.

Within the sky of The Void, there are two celestial objects, the Mind, which resembles a star, and the Eye, which is a simple red eye.

The Mind controls all the molecules of Void, as well as her immense intellegence. If there is more than one Void, the Mind will siphon Void from the floor of The Void as needed.

The Eye, on the other hand, is what processes Void's visual input. It is what parses between Void's sight and rift-sight.

Editors Note: Void technically doesn't have eyes. Her 'eyes' are really just rift-sight, locked on her body.
